About PawTube

PawTube is a nostalgic video platform inspired by the early era of online video between 2005–2012. Simple pages, classic layouts, and a community-driven vibe.

Videos on PawTube use embed codes, keeping everything lightweight, fast, and free.

The First Video Ever Uploaded

The first PawTube video is known as “ed 12/31/1922”.

It’s a silent black-and-white recording filmed on December 31, 1922.

The footage shows a quiet road lined with ordinary trees, no cars, no dialogue — just simple life captured almost a century ago.

This video is considered the origin of PawTube’s archive-style culture.
